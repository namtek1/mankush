About ManKush Enterprises

ManKush Enterprises is your trusted partner in quality tuk-tuk spare parts and accessories.Based in Kenya, we have built a strong reputation for offering reliable, affordable, and genuine tuk-tuk components to keep your business moving.

Our core values include:

  • Integrity: We deliver exactly what we promise.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Your success is our priority.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology to serve you better.
  • Efficiency: We ensure quick service and timely deliveries.

With branches across multiple towns and a dedicated team of attendants, we strive to meet the diverse needs of tuk-tuk owners and drivers across the region.
